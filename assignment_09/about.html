Who is ConcernedApe?

ConcernedApe's Profile Picture

ConcernedApe is the online pseudonym of Eric Barone, an indie game developer best known for creating the popular farming simulation game, "Stardew Valley." He developed the game largely on his own, handling programming, art, music, and design. "Stardew Valley" was released in 2016 and gained widespread acclaim for its charming pixel art style, engaging gameplay, and the freedom it provides players to build and customize their own farms.

ConcernedApe continued to support and update "Stardew Valley" after its initial release, adding new content and features to the game. The success of the game contributed to Barone's reputation as a talented and dedicated indie game developer.

As of now, ConcernedApe is still continuing to update "Stardew Valley" with the upcoming 1.6 "Stardew Valley" update coming soon. Simultaneously, ConcernedApe is also working on his next indie game, "The Haunted Chololatier" with still no release date in sight.
