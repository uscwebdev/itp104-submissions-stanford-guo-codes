betty

Betty is the first of three characters we are introduced to in Taylor Swift's 8th studio album folklore. From what we can gather from the story of the love triangle being told in foklore, Betty was cheated on by her then-boyfriend James. She is known for being quite nostalgic and enjoys being a silent observer on the sidelines, even when she knows that she should step in and fight for herself. She is a very intelligent woman and therefore likes to think before she acts, which in the case of this love triangle, has led her to (potentially) lose "the one".

Every character in folklore has a song, and Betty's song is track 2 on the album and it is entitled cardigan. In this song, Betty reflects on her past failed relationship with James, her ex-boyfriend. She reminisces about being loved and being someone's favorite person, comparing herself to an old cardigan that used to be loved by its wearer. She sings about how she knew what was happening during every step of her and James's relationship, even when people assumed that she was just a naive girl, singing that "when you are young they assume you know nothing".

james

Moving on from Betty, James is obviously the character that should be discussed next. James is the man that every girl could ever want: sweet, kind, a little bit nerdy, but of course he cheated on Betty so that is a major red flag. Nevertheless, James was indeed the ideal boyfriend to Betty when they were in their honeymoon phase. However, his infidelity should not be excused.

The song sung from James's perspective is entitled betty as he is actually singing this song to Betty after the truth is revealed that he cheated on Betty. In his song, James describes the falling out of his and Betty's relationship and how despite cheating on Betty, he still loves and still want to be with Betty. Apparently, Betty is hosting a party soon and James hopes that if he can talk to Betty at her party, he will be able to convince her to stay with him. However, as we learned in Betty's song cardigan, Betty rejects James and that is where their story ends.

augustine

Last but not least, we have the person James cheated on Betty with: Augstine. Now, Augstine is actually not the villain in this whole love triangle; in fact, she did not even know that James was still with Betty when she and James had their summer fling. She truly believed that James loved her and she was deeply in love. Augustine is known for being wistful, cheerful, and trusting, but ultimately naive as her relationship with James was simply just "a moment in time".

In her song entitled august, Augustine describes her summer fling with James and how deep in love she was. She mentions how she "remembered thinking she had [James]", but ultimately, it was all a ruse. She describes how she would "cancel [her] plans just in case [James] called" and she did not care if her plans fell through: she was simply living "for the hope of it all".
