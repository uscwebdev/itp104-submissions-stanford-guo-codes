Farming

Yielding an average of 18.89 gold per day, cranberries are one of the most profitable crops to grow during the fall. Similar to blueberries, cranberries, on harvest, also produce more than one berry per harvest. However, they are quite expensive to purchase as their seeds are 240 gold each at Pierre’s. Still, the profit that you earn from these plants is definitely worth the hole in your pocket on the first day of the month!

Fishing

Although they aren’t the most profitable fish to catch during the fall, I personally believe that these fish are one of the most valuable fish to catch during the fall as they can ONLY be found during the fall. They can be found in the river from 6am to 7pm every day and are worth 75 gold each. You can also use salmon to make salmon dinner in the kitchen, which is loved by Alex and also makes a delicious meal.

Stardew Valley Fair

The Stardew Valley Fair is an annual event that takes place on the 16th of Fall in the game. As a new player, you'll encounter this lively fair, which serves as a fun opportunity to showcase the fruits of your labor, engage in various activities, and earn Star Tokens to exchange for valuable prizes. The primary focus of the fair is the Grange Display, where you can exhibit up to nine items that showcase the diversity of your farm produce and artisan goods. The better the quality and variety of items you display, the more Star Tokens you'll earn. You can turn in these Star Tokens at Pierre’s where you can purchase cosmetics and decorations as well as a juicy Stardrop!

Spirit's Eve

Spirits' Eve is a special event that takes place on the 27th of Fall where you can experience this festival as a unique combination of Halloween-inspired festivities and community interaction. One of the central attractions is the maze, which opens up during the event. The maze is filled with various obstacles and creatures, and navigating it can be both challenging and rewarding. Be sure to explore the maze to discover the hidden golden pumpkin!
