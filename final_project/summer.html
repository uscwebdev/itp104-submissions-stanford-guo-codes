Farming

Blueberries are by the far most profitable crop during the summer. Purchasable from Pierre for 80 gold per seed, each blueberry plant will generate an average of 20.8 gold per day as each blueberry plant is capable of producing up to 3 blueberries per harvest. What more could you possibly want?

Super Cucumber

Fishing

Available at night from 6pm to 2am at the ocean, the purple, elusive, super cucumber is by far the most profitable fish (if you can even call it that) you can catch during the summer. They are at least worth 250 gold per super cucumber, and although they are extremely difficult to catch, the profit you generate from each super cucumber is definitely worth it. Another plus is that the Wizard loves super cucumbers.

Luau

The Luau is a festive beach party that occurs on the 11th of Summer. The Luau takes place on the beach, and it's a great opportunity to engage with the community and contribute to the communal potluck soup. To participate, head to the beach between 9am and 2pm. The Governor, who attends the event, will taste the communal soup, and the quality of the soup depends on the items contributed by the townspeople. You'll have the chance to add an item to the soup to enhance its flavor. Choose wisely, as the success of the soup affects everyone's mood. Different items have varying effects, and selecting a well-liked ingredient can lead to positive reactions from the villagers.

Dance of the Moonlight Jellies

Dance of the Moonlight Jellies

The Dance of the Moonlight Jellies is a magical and tranquil event that takes place on the 28th of Summer. The event revolves around the appearance of the rare and beautiful Moonlight Jellies, luminescent jellyfish that visit Pelican Town's shores during this time of year.During the event, the entire town gathers on the beach to marvel at the Moonlight Jellies as they gracefully glide through the water. The scene is accompanied by soothing music, contributing to the serene atmosphere. Unlike other festivals, the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies doesn't involve active participation or tasks for the player. Instead, it's a contemplative and visually stunning experience that highlights the game's focus on nature and the changing seasons.
